## Reasoning

- **Beverage brands** (San Pellegrino, Peroni): substitution boundaries in PT foodservice — kitchens order by brand.
- **Charcuterie brands** (Rovagnati, Rigamonti): procurement identity; culinary noun is product type + grade.
- **Distributor suffixes** (Sorrentino, Amoruso): same pattern as Simonetta/Caputo — strip.
- **Mancini**: artisan pasta mill — strip unless restaurant stocks multiple paccheri brands.
