# Brand Retention Framework

**Date:** 2026-06-15

---

## ALWAYS_REMOVE

Rovagnati, Rigamonti, Arrigoni, Assaporami, Sorrentino, Amoruso, alconfirsta

Already implemented: De Cecco, Baladin, Simonetta, Caputo, Toschi

**Reasoning:** Charcuterie/cheese brands and distributor suffixes are procurement identity — culinary noun + DOP/grade is the kitchen category.

---

## ALWAYS_KEEP

San Pellegrino, Peroni

DOP, IGP, beverage serving formats (75cl, 33cl)

**Reasoning:** Beverage brands are substitution boundaries in PT foodservice. Protected designations are legally and culinarily meaningful.
