## Decision

### **MANUAL_REVIEW_SUFFICIENT** (launch)

- All 21 Italian rows are ACCEPTABLE+ (not EMPTY)
- Review & Create usable at 90.6% food-only
- Manual edit of ~8–10 suggestions feasible at current volume

### **IMPLEMENT_ITALIAN_SUPPLIER_CLEANUP** (optional Phase 4)

- Justified as narrow **2–3 day pass** when Emporio/Mammafiore/Bocconcino invoices are routine
- Not a full ontology build
- Quality polish, not usability unlock

---

## Summary

Patterns are **real and repeatable**, not isolated edge cases — but **volume is small**. Automation improves a **meaningful share of Italian items (38–48%)** without touching matching, pricing, or schema. Base decision on measured frequency: launch with manual review; automate when supplier volume justifies the effort.
